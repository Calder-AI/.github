<h3 align="center">The Foundation of Spatial Intelligence</h3>

<p align="center">

Current AI models lack the spatial intelligence needed to navigate the real world. The bottleneck isn't compute or algorithms — it's data. High-quality 3D sensor data from real environments simply doesn't exist at scale on the internet.

**Calder fills that gap.** We capture, process, and deliver the essential training data for general-purpose robotics and spatial AI.
